If you are the DSL member hosting a social night, the following is a
list of the things to mention.

- Welcome everyone
- Explain what DSL is
- We are member supported (remind visitors that if they have enjoyed their time
  at DSL, we would appreciate a small donation
- Introductory membership
- Fridge, with beer and softdrinks
- We now going around the room, introducing ourselves and describing what
  you like to hack on
- Ask the room if they have a 15 - 20 minute talk that they would like to
  present in the future, or a topic the want to hear more about
- If there is a talk, introduce the talk

Additionally, the host should make sure that someone is greeting any
new visitors and welcoming them to the space.
